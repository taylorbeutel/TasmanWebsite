About Tasman

Did you know that Abel Tasman was a real explorer? He was the first person to discover Tasmania and is the reason that is called that name today! The image on this page is actually a picture of the tree that Tasman first tied his boat to when he arrived in Tasmania! Quickly after exploring the land, Tasman decided to plant 10 apple trees to see if the land was fertile. Unfortunately when he returned many years later, only 2 trees remained.
