Royal Tasmanian Botanical Gardens

This garden has been mantained by the locals for centuries as the first Botanical Gardens in Australia! There is such a great variety of plants here so I would highly recommend making the trip!

Fresh Food

While exploring the gardens I came across a section which contained different types of food! After a long hard day of adventuring, this was most welcome! The most fascinating was the cherry tree which was able to grow in two forms, free standing or climbing up a support. Although these trees did not have any fruit on their branches when I visited, there should be some there when you go!

Neat Gardens

After caring for these gardens for such a long time, the local people have mastered the art of creating beautiful and neat gardens. I discovered that the garden pictured, contains the flower called a tulip which must be replanted each year so that flowers appear during Spring!
